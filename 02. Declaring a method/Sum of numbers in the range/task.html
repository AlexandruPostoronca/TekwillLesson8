Sum of numbers in the range

Implement a method sumInRange for calculating the sum of numbers in the range from (inclusive), to (exclusive). Mind the type of the return value.

Sample Input 1: 

1 2

Sample Output 1: 

1

Sample Input 2: 

5 5

Sample Output 2: 

0

Sample Input 3: 

10 15

Sample Output 3: 

60
